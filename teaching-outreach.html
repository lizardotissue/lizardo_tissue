Teaching & Outreach

Inspiring the next generation of scientists and engineers

Teaching Experience

Teaching Assistant & Lab Head in Tissue Engineering

Status: Current

As the head of laboratory practices in the Introduction to Tissue Engineering course, I guide students in key techniques such as 2D and 3D cell culture, tissue segmentation via specialized software for pre-surgical printing, biofabrication, and cellular decellularization, laying a strong foundation in regenerative medicine.

  • Topics Covered: 2D & 3D Cell Culture, Tissue Segmentation, Biofabrication, Decellularization
  • Course Level: Undergraduate
University Lecturer & Lab Head in Biomaterials

Status: Current

Since August 2023, I have served as the lead instructor and lab head in the Biomaterials course for Biomedical Engineering students. My teaching focuses on practical applications in biomaterials, guiding students in synthesizing hydrogels, understanding the basics of bioprinting, surface technology, plasma coating processes, and tribology.

  • Topics Covered: Hydrogel Synthesis, Basic Bioprinting, Surface Technology, Plasma Coating, Tribology
  • Course Level: Undergraduate
