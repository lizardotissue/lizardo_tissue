Lizardo Kendy Torres Ayala

Biomedical Engineer | MSc. in Materials Science and Engineering

About Me

Research Focus

I specialize in Biomedical Engineering and Biomaterials Science, exploring the mechanical properties of biomaterials to optimize their use in medical applications. My work includes studying their strength, elasticity, and durability.

Innovative Projects

Throughout my career, I've worked on developing innovative medical devices such as a multifunctional wheelchair for children with cerebral palsy and contributed to bioprinting with open-source hardware.

Beyond Biomedicine

With roots in Cusco and a background in Materials Science, I have a passion for archaeology and bioarchaeology. I am keen to apply modern materials science to study ancient artifacts and sustainable solutions.

Mentorship & Knowledge Sharing

I am committed to mentoring future scientists in Latin America, sharing knowledge and fostering growth in the scientific community. Let's connect if you share an interest in advancing materials science.
