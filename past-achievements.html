Design of a Multifunctional Specialized Wheelchair for Cerebral Palsy in Children

This paper presents a design for a wheelchair with multiple functionalities, including sitting, standing, and stretcher modes for children with cerebral palsy. The mechanism uses only one actuator and has been evaluated for its effectiveness in ensuring comfort and adaptability.

Conference: 44th Annual International Conference of the IEEE Engineering in Medicine & Biology Society (EMBC)

Location: Scottish Event Campus, Glasgow, UK, July 11-15, 2022

DOI: 10.1109/EMBC48229.2022.9871696
